Tips for East African Groceries

1. Watch Flour Options: Wholemeal Maize meal (like un-refined Unga) secures direct Grade A due to high fiber, whereas highly-refined premium meal lands at Grade B/C.

2. Kenyan Teas are naturally Grade A: Plain tea leaves like Ketepa contain no added sodium, calories or sugar. Drink without sweeteners to retain their benefits.

3. Low-Fat Dairy swaps: Swapping whole-fat milk packets to Brookside slim or skimmed milk directly decreases saturated fats, elevating your grade from Grade C to Grade B.
